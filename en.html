Research Summary & Recruitment

Our research group utilizes cutting-edge fluorescence imaging technologies to elucidate biological phenomena based on spatiotemporal dynamics. Specifically, our primary themes include the development of novel epigenome editing technologies, the regulatory mechanisms of genomic imprinting, and the elucidation of transcriptional regulation mechanisms driven by 3D genome architecture.

We are actively recruiting PhD students and researchers (Postdocs) to join our team in developing unique research that fuses "Epigenomics" and "Imaging."

Dokkyo Medical University is located in a serene environment rich in nature, offering a perfect setting to immerse oneself in research. We provide an optimal environment for building your career, equipped with a comprehensive common equipment center, abundant resources unique to a medical school, and a robust research support system. We invite those with the ambition to pioneer science from a unique perspective to contact us.
